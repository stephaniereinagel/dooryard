> **Core rule when priorities conflict:**
> *Keep animals alive → keep kids safe → keep Friday's stand ready.* Everything else waits.

## 🕗 Every shift, every day

### 1. Arrival (8:00–8:15, ~15 min)

- [ ] Park in designated spot: **[CONFIRM]**
- [ ] Check the **priority whiteboard** in the **[CONFIRM: mudroom / barn / coop]**
- [ ] Read yesterday's shift log (in the clipboard)
- [ ] Quick property loop — walk past coop, duck area, garden, stand area. Just *look*. Note anything unusual (sick-looking bird, obvious damage, something that wasn't there before).

### 2. Daily core chores (8:15–9:30, ~75 min)

These happen **every shift** in this order. See `task-standards.md` for how "done well" looks for each.

- [ ] **Chickens**: visual check (all up and moving, no obvious injuries), refill feed, refill water, collect eggs
- [ ] **Ducks**: visual check, refill feed, **swap & refill water** (ducks muck water fast), collect eggs
- [ ] **Egg handling**: wipe off visible debris (dry-brush, no washing unless noted), sort into cartons, place in **[CONFIRM: mudroom fridge / stand cooler]**, write count on the log sheet
- [ ] **Garden walk-through** (10 min, *observation only*): check irrigation is running if it should be, note any pest damage, note any bed that needs water/weeding/harvest. **What to DO in the garden comes from the MyGardenCoach app — not this walk-through.** The walk-through is just so you catch problems early.

### 3. Day-specific work (9:30–11:30, ~2 hrs)

👉 *See the day below.*

### 4. Backlog pull (if time remains before 11:50)

Go to `backlogs.md` and pull from **Maintenance** (top of list) until 11:50.

### 5. Wrap-up (11:50–12:00, ~10 min)

- [ ] Fill in today's **shift log** (`shift-log.md`)
- [ ] Return tools to their spots
- [ ] Update the priority whiteboard: cross off what's done, add any new flags
- [ ] Text Stephanie **only** if something is urgent (see "When to text immediately" below). Otherwise everything goes in the log.

## 📅 Day-specific work

### MONDAY — Garden focus

After daily core chores (9:30–11:30):

1. **Open MyGardenCoach** → https://mygardencoach.netlify.app (log in with the family account Stephanie gave you). This is your daily source of truth for the garden: transplants, sowing, harvesting, weeding, watering, pest actions.
2. Work the coach app's task list in order of priority until 11:30.
3. If you finish the app's list with time remaining → pull from **Maintenance → Garden/Infrastructure** in `backlogs.md`.
4. If the coach app says something you disagree with or can't do (e.g. soil too wet to transplant), log it and move on — don't force it.

**Monday goal:** every item MyGardenCoach queued for today is done or has a logged reason it wasn't.

### WEDNESDAY — Animals + stand prep

After daily core chores (9:30–11:30):

- **Deep animal task** (rotate weekly — see list below)
  - Week 1 of month: clean chicken coop (bedding refresh)
  - Week 2: clean duck house + duck water buckets scrub
  - Week 3: nest boxes + roosts check, cobweb down
  - Week 4: feed storage audit + pest check (mice, spilled grain)
- **Kitchen/stand prep help** (when Stephanie needs it): package granola, label cookie bags, prep egg cartons, flower jars rinsed and ready
- If no stand prep needed: pull from **Maintenance** backlog

**Wednesday goal:** animals are cared for deeper than a daily check, and Friday is 50% set up before you leave.

### FRIDAY — Farm stand day (March–October)

The stand is **self-serve.** Your job is to get it **open, visible, and announced** — not to man it. Once it's live, Stephanie handles closedown later.

8:00–8:15 · Arrival + **quick** animal check (feed + water + eggs; skip deep stuff). Check MyGardenCoach for anything that MUST be harvested today for the stand.

8:15–9:30 · **Harvest for stand** — flowers, produce, final egg collection. Count and pack eggs into cartons. Bundle flowers in jars of water. Wash/dry any produce.

9:30–10:30 · **Bake-goods + packaging** — stage baked items Stephanie prepped, label, price. (If Stephanie has the list on the whiteboard, follow that; otherwise use last week's lineup as default.)

10:30–11:30 · **Stand setup** — haul tote + products to stand, set tables/shade/signage/display, cash box with float, QR code printout. See `task-standards.md §7` for the full "done looks like this."

11:30–11:45 · **Open the stand** — flip the "OPEN" sign, put out the road-facing signs, double-check visibility from a passing car. Then post to Facebook (template: **[CONFIRM: link or script]**). Photo of the day's lineup, list prices, tag local groups if that's the pattern.

11:45–12:00 · **Wrap-up log** — fill in shift log, note what's on the stand and rough inventory count (helps Stephanie at closedown), tools away, leave.

**Stand closedown:** not your job during your scheduled shift. Stephanie handles in the afternoon/evening. If she ever asks you to close, see `task-standards.md §8`.

**Friday, off-season (Nov–Feb):** revert to Monday-style work — MyGardenCoach task list first, then maintenance/projects backlog.

## 📣 Communication rules

### When to text Stephanie immediately

- Injured/dead animal, predator sighting, or escape
- Kid safety issue
- Serious property damage (flooding, fire, broken water line)
- Stranger on the property
- You're hurt

### When to put it in the log (not text)

- Supply running low
- Something minor is broken
- A bed needs attention
- You have a question that can wait for Friday 11:45 wrap-up
- You finished a project and need what's next

### Weekly check-in

**Friday 11:45–12:00** — 10-minute in-person with Stephanie. Review the week's log, she answers batched questions, adjusts the whiteboard for next week.
